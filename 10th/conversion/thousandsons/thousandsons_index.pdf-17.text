THOUSAND SONS RHINO
Many of the Rhino APCs employed by the Thousand Sons are ancient
vehicles, their once-noble machine spirits saturated by the malice
of the warp, their hulls mutated and discoloured by the energies of
unfettered change. Still they bear the foot soldiers of Tzeentch swiftly
into battle, guns hammering as they go.
